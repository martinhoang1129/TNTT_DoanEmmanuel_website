About Us

Our Mission

The heart and purpose of Đoàn Emmanuel

VEYM

Vietnamese Eucharistic Youth Movement

The Vietnamese Eucharistic Youth Movement (Thiếu Nhi Thánh Thể – TNTT) is a Catholic youth organization dedicated to forming the whole person — spiritually, intellectually, physically, and socially — through Eucharistic spirituality. Our movement is centered on Jesus Christ present in the Eucharist.

Đoàn Emmanuel, named in honor of Our Lady of La Vang, is part of the St. Benedict League (Liên Đoàn Biển Đức) and serves Vietnamese Catholic families in our community. Since our founding over 40 years ago, we have nurtured generations of faithful young Catholics.

"To form young Vietnamese Catholics deeply rooted in the Eucharist, guided by Our Lady of La Vang, growing in faith, knowledge, and service."

Our Team

The Huynh Trưởng leaders who dedicate their time and hearts

Our Huynh Trưởng (youth leaders) are the backbone of Đoàn Emmanuel. These dedicated volunteers undergo rigorous formation training (Cấp 1, Cấp 2, Cấp 3) to serve our children with the highest level of commitment, faith, and love.

Leadership

BCH Members

The Ban Chấp Hành (BCH) oversees the operations of our Đoàn and ensures faithful formation according to VEYM guidelines.

Formation

Huynh Trưởng Training

Our leaders are certified through a multi-level training process, growing in their calling to serve youth with excellence and faith.

Want to join our team? If you are 18+ and feel called to serve Vietnamese Catholic youth, we welcome you to explore becoming a Huynh Trưởng.

Our History

Four decades of faith, community, and service

1985

Founded

Đoàn Emmanuel was established by a small group of Vietnamese Catholic families committed to preserving faith and culture for their children in the United States.

Early Years

Doan Truong History

Through the years, our Đoàn grew steadily, establishing all five ngành divisions and building strong connections within the Liên Đoàn Biển Đức regional chapter.

Annual Tradition

Trại Lavang — Annual Summer Camp

Our annual Lavang summer camp has become a beloved tradition — a highlight of the year where hundreds of youth and leaders gather for faith, fun, and formation. Lavang XXV marked our 40th anniversary.

Today

40 Years and Counting

Today our Đoàn continues to thrive, sending leaders to national competitions, hosting retreats and camps, and forming the next generation of Vietnamese Catholic leaders with the same spirit as our founders.

Ngành

Ấu Nhi

Seedling Division · Ages 6–9

Ấu Nhi

"Ngoan" — Obedience

Our youngest family members, the Ấu Nhi motto is Ngoan, which translates to "obedience." The các em ngành Ấu wear green — the color of a young and growing tree, representing youth, innocence, and new life in Christ.

Ấu Nhi formation focuses on simple prayers, love for the Eucharist, and learning to be good, obedient children who reflect God's love in their daily lives at home and at school.

Thiếu Nhi

Search Division · Ages 10–12

Thiếu Nhi

"Hy Sinh" — Sacrifice

The Thiếu Nhi motto is Hy Sinh, meaning "sacrifice." The các em wear blue — the color of the endless sea and sky, representing the vast search for identity and the courage to sacrifice for what matters.

Thiếu Nhi formation deepens the understanding of the Eucharist, builds community, and encourages young Catholics to embrace sacrifice as a way of growing closer to Christ.

Nghĩa Sĩ

Companion Division · Ages 13–15

Nghĩa Sĩ

"Chinh Phục" — Conquer

The Nghĩa Sĩ motto is Chinh Phục, meaning "conquer." The các em wear yellow — the color of sunrise and sunset, representing the conquering light overcoming darkness, and the courage of youth in their faith journey.

Formation at this level addresses the challenges of adolescence, identity, and deepening one's personal relationship with Christ and the Church community.

Hiệp Sĩ

Eucharistic Knights Division · Ages 16–17

Hiệp Sĩ

"Dấn Thân" — Plunge into Service

The Hiệp Sĩ motto is Dấn Thân, meaning "plunging headlong into the world." The các em wear brown — the color of service and the earth — reminding them to stay grounded while actively serving those around them.

Hiệp Sĩ are invited to take on real service roles within the Church and community, preparing them to transition into the Huynh Trưởng leadership path.

Huynh Trưởng

Youth Leaders · Ages 18+

Huynh Trưởng

"Phụng Sự" — Service

The Huynh Trưởng motto is Phụng Sự, meaning "service." They wear red — the color symbolizing love, service, sacrifice, and responsibility. Huynh Trưởng are the spiritual older siblings to all the younger ngành.

Huynh Trưởng undergo multi-level formation training (Cấp 1, Cấp 2, Cấp 3) and are commissioned to help younger members grow in faith and grow closer to God through example and leadership.
